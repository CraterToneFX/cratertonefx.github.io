DIY Links

This page has links to a wealth of information for any level pedal builder / electronics enthusiast.
For now I'll list a bunch, but it will grow and get sorted better as time goes on.

You can find most of the vero layout circuits listed on this site with this search engine PedalLayouts.com
All images used on this site are the property of the original owners and are used to show the circuit and where they came from.

Comments are available through Github on each post here, if you don't have a Github account you can get one for free at Github.com.

Links

amzfx

AMZ is a resource with information about diy guitar effects pedals, stompboxes, audio circuits and tips on how to build them.

Analog Alchemy

This website contains information for electronics hobbyists such as calculators and schematics.

DIYstompboxes

DIYstompboxes.com is one of the oldest and best known sites for learning how to mod and build your own guitar effects pedal.
There are many members from around the world and there is a wealth of info in the forum and on this site.

Paul In The Lab

Electronics Projects For Hobbyists Makers And Hackers With Stripboard Layouts

Making Easy Circuits

Learn and build electronic circuits

Paul Falstad

Math and physics applets, circuit simulator and more.

ElectronicsTutorials

Electronics-tutorials.ws (founded by Wayne Storr) is one of the longest-running and most respected free educational resources for learning basic electronics and electrical engineering.

runoffgroove

runoffgroove.com projects are provided free-of-charge to builders for personal, non-commercial use.

Schematics Online

Free schematics and diagrams database.

SchematicsForFree

Electronics Info and Schematics database.

Hobby Circuits

Completely free circuit diagrams and electronics projects database!

Elliott Sound Products

Professional results for the Do-It-Yourself enthusiast

General Guitar Gadgets

Schematics, wiring diagrams and detailed instructions for the best DIY Stompbox effects Projects on the internet.

MadBeanPedals

Madbeanpedals (run by Brian based in Nashville, TN) is one of the most highly regarded creators in the DIY guitar effects community.
They provide high-quality, meticulously documented Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) and project guides that allow musicians to build their own clone and modded boutique effects

RED Free Circuit Designs

RED Free Circuit Designs (often referred to as RED Circuits) is an online repository of electronic schematics created by Italian hobbyist Flavio Dellepiane.

Wampler DIY

Wampler DIY is a platform created by Brian Wampler, founder of Wampler Pedals, dedicated to helping guitarists learn how to build, modify, and understand their own effects pedals.

LTspice|Analog Devices

LTspice® is a powerful, fast, and free SPICE simulator software, schematic capture and waveform viewer with enhancements and models for improving the simulation of analog circuits.

Experimentalists Anonymous

An online resource and information repository for people interested in building their own audio circuits since 2003.

Stomp Box Schematics

Schematics, circuits, PCBs and veroboards for guitar effects pedals.

Super-Freq

The site provides resources, project files, and information for soldering and assembling custom stompboxes.
