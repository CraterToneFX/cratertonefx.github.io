About CraterToneFX

CraterToneFX is a collection of notes, experiments, and design write-ups focused on guitar pedals, a little on amps, and DIY audio electronics.
I'm not an expert in any way, just a hobbyist hoping to help other hobbyists.
Expect schematics and vero layouts that I have had success with.

You can find most of the vero layout circuits listed on this site with this search engine PedalLayouts.com
