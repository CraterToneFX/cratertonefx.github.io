This is another circuit that uses the LM386 chip, some like them some hate them.
There were a couple layouts on Guitar FX Layouts depending on what transistor you used.
I used a 2N5457. As far as the LM386, it looks like it may depend on what version chip you have -1, -3, -4 if you have weird noise or not.
I tried a few different -1 and a -4, a couple of them had some artifacting at the end of a note ringing out. The others sounded good.
