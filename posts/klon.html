Klon Centaur

April 18, 2026 · Overdrive

This is another circuit that made it's way to my pedal board, but has since been removed. Nothing wrong with the circuit, just not my cup of tea.
The circuit I built sounded a little muffled, could be the clippers I used or the op amps, I don't know. Maybe that's how they sound.
I used NOS Russian D9D diodes for clippers, TL072 op amps for both IC's (did have an NE5532 for the 2nd one for a bit)
and changed the tone pot to B20K to give it a little more brightness at the top of the range.
Update 04/28/2026 - I messed with this circuit a little more by adding 2 more Russian diodes in series so it's 2x2.
This helped more with the darkness and gives it a little more volume, I like it much more with that addition.
