BSIAB II

July 01, 2026 · Distortion

Brown Sound In A Box II with Contour control. This circuit sounds awesome, of course me being an old guy might slightly biased.
It has that sound from classic rock days from later 70's into early 80's for me anyway.
Plenty of adjustability to get what you're looking for.



I used MMBFJ201 JFETs throughout so I think it has more gain than it probably should.
I have the gain turned down quite a bit. Maybe that's why it's not on my board, but I do love the sound of it.
