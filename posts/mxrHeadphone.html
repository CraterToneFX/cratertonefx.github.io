The MXR Headphone Amp

I just built this one the other day and it's replacing the Headbanger Headphone Amp as my desk pedal tester / playing at the desk headphone amp.

The Headbanger Amp is a very good headphone amp, more than enough output and sounds great.
For playing guitar through it it's fine most of the time but if pushing too many pedals at once or just loud and long it sometimes will deteriorate the signal.
The MXR so far has not done that and I think has a more guitar amp sounding output.
